About

Memories syncs your Mac photo library to your iPhone privately. The Mac companion app reads your Photos library and uploads encrypted records to your personal iCloud container. The iPhone app downloads and organizes them with full privacy features.

Zero third-party SDKs. Zero analytics. Your photos stay yours.
